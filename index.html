Hosts of Slice Of Tri

Maurya Couvares

Maurya grew up as a competitive swimmer, and this summer is her seventh season as a triathlete. She is completely in love with the sport, and enjoys being part of a community of people who share her passion.

Ben Kessel

The variety of triathlons is what keeps Ben hooked to the sport: the technicality of swimming, the endurance/power of cycling, and the mental game and rhythm of running. His favorite race was his first race: the Staten Island sprint. He is a fitness coach, and a coach of the Brooklyn Triathlon Club.

Mat Ward

Mat moved to Brooklyn in 2013 from London. He's been competing in triathlons since 2011, and was excited to find a community of triathletes in Brooklyn. His club-mates are his New York family and his fondest memory is hearing his friends cheers when running the NYC marathon.
